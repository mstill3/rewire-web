About Us

Riding on an airplane to Alabama in Decemeber of 2019, I had the idea to create an app that would help people struggling with negative thoughts. This project idea means a lot to me and is something I hold close to my heart. Personally I wanted this app to exist to not only help myself but also help others including my own family and friends.

I am a huge supporter of advocating for mental health awareness. I know everyone struggles with automatic negative thoughts time to time so this is an app whose entire purpose is to help.
